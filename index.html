How to Play

🎲 Roll the dice to move across the board from square 1 to 30.

🪜 Land on a ladder to climb up and earn +5 points.

🐍 Land on a snake to slide down and lose -1 point. Each snake only triggers once per game.

❓ Land on a trivia square to answer an AI question. Correct = +3 points.

📖 Every snake and ladder square reveals a "Did You Know" insight in the right panel.

🏆 Reach square 30 to finish! Your maturity level depends on your score and number of rolls.

🥇 Gold: Score ≥ 10 & Rolls ≤ 20  |  🥈 Silver: Score ≥ 8 or Rolls ≤ 20  |  🥉 Bronze: Everything else
